

   # Edgework: "Trace your edges."
    Minimal social network for cozy, cyberpunk vibes. it is a social network inspired by Tumblr and Pinterest.
        
    It lets users:

    Register and login (with pre-generated users via Faker)

     Create posts and comments

     Like posts

    Follow other users

    View a feed of posts from people they follow

    Explore a network graph of their social connections

    Supports both a terminal-based TUI and a cozy GUI for quick social prototyping.
